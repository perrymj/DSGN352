Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can (6 oz) tuna, drained and flaked
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 teaspoons chopped dill pickle
  • 1/4th cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Place bread slices in the oven to toast while it preheats
  3. In a small bowl, mix together tuna, mayonnaise, salt, balsamic vinegar and mustard until well blended
  4. Remove bread from the oven, and pile the tuna mixture onto one slice. Sprinkle cheese over the other slice of bread.
  5. Bake for 7 minutes, or until cheese is melted and tuna is heated through
  6. Place the cheese side of the sandwich on top of the tuna side. Cut in half and serve immediately.
